St. Ignatius was born on October 23, 1491. St. Ignatius grew up in the village of Loyola. His mother died when was young. He had dreams of becoming a great leader for his people and to be well known. When he was eighteen, he joined the Spanish military in wars against the French. Still wanting to be well known, he started to call himself Ignatius. In one battle he got hit by a cannonball, and doctors performed many surgeries to save his life.
